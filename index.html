Our Why

Excellyte was born from resilience and gratitude. After losing a leg and learning to walk again, I worked long days in the Arizona heat and discovered I had compensatory sweating. I was leaving harsh white salt stains on my clothes and nearly hit heat exhaustion more than once. I searched for something clean and effective — real salt, real fruit, no added sugar — that could replace what I was losing and keep me safe. Excellyte exists to help others in real heat and real life. It’s hydration with purpose — and with gratitude.
