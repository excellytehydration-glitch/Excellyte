FAQ

What counts as a “daily” post?

One short video per calendar day for 30 consecutive days. If you miss a day, restart the streak and keep going — we’re cheering for you.

Which platforms are allowed?

TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts. You can switch between them, just keep the streak daily.

Do I have to show my face?

Nope. Voiceover, captioned text, or even a simple clip with on-screen text works — just share three things you’re grateful for.

How do I claim the reward?

When you finish your streak, go to claim.html and submit your handle, start/end dates, and a proof link (playlist, hashtag page, or sample posts). We’ll verify and contact you for shipping.

Is there any cost?

No purchase necessary. Reward limited to one pouch per person per quarter while supplies last.
